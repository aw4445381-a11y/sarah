My story

I grew up between hymns and the hum of family kitchens — stories simmering, worship rising. In Brazil, I learned color; in Portugal, I learned patience. My heart is set on mission work and gentle creativity: singing, painting, and learning languages to meet people where they are.

I love quiet Saturdays with a sketchbook, bread baking in the oven, and a cozy playlist of older worship songs. One day, I hope for a small home with wood floors, quilt blankets, a lazy cat, a gentle dog, and a table always ready for friends.

Journey highlights

  1. Brazilian beginnings

    Learned harmonies at church with my mom; painted my first canvas daisies; fell in love with color.

  2. New roots in Portugal

    Joined a youth worship team; started journaling Bible verses; discovered a love for baking & hosting.

  3. Languages & mission

    Studying English & Spanish (and bits of Italian!) to serve cross-culturally and share the Gospel.

  4. Art with purpose

    Painting small florals for friends, creating scripture cards, and saving for a community art day.
